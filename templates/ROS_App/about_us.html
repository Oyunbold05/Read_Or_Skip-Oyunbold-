About Us

The inspiration for this project was our love for literature, some of us are avid readers who enjoy a cozy reading afternoon with a hot cup of tea and the rain outside. Some of us are not but are slowly getting more curious about what books have to offer and just dabbing into new genres. We wanted to create a cozy space for people to explore new books that, for some of us, feel like home and to inspire others to take the plunge and pick up that new book. They can do what Gandalf said to Bilbo in the Shire: "The world is not in your books and maps, it's out there..." after exiting the app. Until then, hurry up, the tea is about to get cold!

We also felt the apps that are on the market currently lack the cozy aspect. Also we introduced a new feature for the user and it's called "Read or Skip", after they finished their book they can add it to the piles depending if they liked it or not. We also feel that life is to short to read all the books we want so that's why we added a TBR (To Be Read) so they can feel closer to their reading goal.
